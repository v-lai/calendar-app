About

A calendar that helps track your moods each day. Users can input moods and colors onto a calendar for that particular day.

Inspired by We Feel Fine, a project by Jonathan Harris & Sepandar Kamvar, based an exploration of human emotion on a global scale that harvested human feelings from weblogs.
